Privacy Policy

Your information is used to plan your travel, and nothing else.

T&M Travel ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we use it.

What we collect

When you submit a contact or planning form, we collect the details you provide, such as your name, email, phone number, and information about the trip you'd like to plan. We may also collect basic, anonymous usage data (such as pages visited) to understand how the site is used.

How we use it

We use the information you share to respond to your inquiry, plan and book your travel, and stay in touch about your trip. We do not sell your information.

Who we share it with

To plan and book travel, we may share necessary details with the travel suppliers and partners involved in your trip (for example, cruise lines, resorts, tour operators, and booking systems). We share only what's needed to arrange your travel.

Cookies and analytics

This site may use cookies and analytics tools to measure traffic and improve the experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices

You can ask us to update or delete the information you've shared at any time by contacting us at the details below.
