Common questions

Good to know before we start.

What kinds of trips do you plan?

Cruises, family vacations, luxury escapes, international travel, and just about anything in between. If it's a trip, I can help you plan it.

I don't live in Bend. Can you still help me?

Absolutely. I'm based in Central Oregon but plan travel for people anywhere. We work together by phone, email, and video, whatever is easiest for you.

What does it cost to work with T&M Travel?

For many trips, my planning support comes at little or no added cost to you because I'm supported by the travel partners I book with. For more complex custom itineraries, I'll always be upfront about any planning fee before we begin.

How far ahead should I reach out?

The earlier the better for the best availability and pricing, but don't let timing stop you. I'm happy to help with last-minute trips too.
