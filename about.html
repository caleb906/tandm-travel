Planning travel on your own is overwhelming.

Planning travel can be confusing and stressful. There are too many choices on flights, hotels, and entertainment, and it's easy to feel buried before the trip even starts. You are meant to enjoy a vacation, not dread the planning of it.

T&M Travel makes it easier by building a personalized travel plan, so you can stop stressing about your vacation and start enjoying it. You should have confidence in your travel advisor, and someone in your corner from the first idea to the day you get home.

It's simple. You reach out, I build a plan that's personalized to you, and then you enjoy your vacation. Everything from how you travel to how you spend your days is shaped around what you actually want. No call centers, no being passed around, just one person who knows your trip.

The result? You stop wasting hours stressing about planning, and you get to enjoy a vacation of a lifetime.
